###Features###
* 32bit ARM TM4C123GH6PM, 80 MHz (64pin)
* 256KB Flash, 32KB RAM
* 43 I/O Pins
* 16 PWM
* 12 Analog Input (12bit)
* 4x SPI, 4x I2C, 2x CAN
* 2 buttons 1 RGB LED
