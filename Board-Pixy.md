###Features###
* NXP  LPC4330, dual core (M4F, M0), both cores @ 204 MHz
* 1024KB flash ROM, 264KB RAM
* camera - Omnivision OV9715, 1/4", 1280x800 
* 2 servos
* 1 user switch
* USB, SPI, I2C, UART
* 140mA consumption
