###Features###
* STM32F401VCT6 168MHz (100pin)
* 256KB flashROM, 64KB RAM
* 3axis gyro L3GD20, 3axis accelerometerLSM303DLHC, 3axis compassMP45DT02
* mems microphopne, audio amp CS43L22
* 8 LEDs
* USB OTG
